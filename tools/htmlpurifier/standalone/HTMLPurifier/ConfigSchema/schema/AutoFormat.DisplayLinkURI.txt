AutoFormat.DisplayLinkURI
TYPE: bool
VERSION: 3.2.0
DEFAULT: false
--DESCRIPTION--
<p>
  This directive turns on the in-text display of URIs in &lt;a&gt; tags, and disables
  those links. For example, <a href="http://example.com">example</a> becomes
  example (<a>http://example.com</a>).
</p>
